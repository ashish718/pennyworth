Audit and Advisory

Audit assists in the establishment of ‘best practices’ which in turn leads to sound internal controls within the company. Audit also aids in enhancing the efficiency in operations which leads to performance improvement and profit of the company. Audit helps businesses in multiple ways. Notable among those are mentioned below.

  • Adds value in solving the issues faced by businesses by providing insight on the effectiveness of governance, risk management, and internal control processes.
  • Post-audit recommendations help in fortifying controls, enabling efficient bookkeeping and building proficient accounting practices.
  • Depicts a clear picture of the prevailing financial situation of the company and provides valuable insights on future scenario which can prepare companies to handle upcoming conditions in a much better way.
  • Helps in gaining investor confidence as audit report is a valuable tool for financial institutions and investment houses to estimate the valuation of a business firm.

Personal & Income Tax Planning

Tax planning is an essential part of an individual. Not being able to adequately take advantage of the permissible deductions and exemption provisions provided by the government can negatively impact an individual’s finances. While timely payment of the due taxes comes back to you in the form of the development of the country, it is wise to keep tax liability to the minimum and optimize finances wherever possible.

At Penny Worth Consultants, we provide smart tax planning solutions and help you in understanding how tax planning is an integral part of an overall financial plan of individual. We also create efficient tax planning to maximize benefits while helping individuals stay compliant at the same time. The Income Tax Act of India, 1961 elaborates the direct taxes levied on individuals. Most of the individuals fail to assess their tax liability and end up paying unnecessary taxes or opt for inefficient tax savings.

We ensure that you maximize the tax saving instruments made available to you. Our experienced tax consultants adopt a proactive approach to maximize tax benefits for you and offer end-to-end tax solutions.

We offer a wide range tax planning and preparation services including:

Tax Planning for Salaried Individuals
Tax planning for a salaried individual means an analysis of the financial situation, with an eye on tax efficiency. Efficient tax planning helps a salaried person use tax exemptions, and deductions to reduce tax liability while not evading tax. Salaried individuals must know how to plan investments in a way that will minimize their tax drain.
We advise salaried individuals on the tax implications and offer expert assistance in filing returns. Our scope of services also includes complete support in claiming a refund of excess tax paid.

Capital Gains Tax
Any gain earned from the sale or transfer of a capital asset is a capital gain. This profit falls under the category ‘income’. Therefore, an individual is liable to pay tax for the amount. The tax in this regard has to be paid in the year of the transfer of the capital asset. Capital gains tax can either be short-term or long-term.
This tax component can sometimes significantly add to tax outgo. We offer expert services in helping reduce capital gains tax in a legal manner and help in lawful claiming of the applicable exemptions.

Tax on House Property
Owning a house property comes with tax responsibilities. Paying taxes annually for the house property is one of them. This house property can be anything from your home, your home building rented out as an office, a shop, or a parking place.
Your house property is liable for tax under the laws of the Government of India. And tax on house property is an important component on which significant amount tax can be levied. We offer services in tax optimization on such properties and help in staying compliant.

Tax on Agricultural Income
Income from sources such as farming land and commercial produce comes under agricultural income in India. Further, income from sale of replanted trees, sale of seeds, growing flowers and creepers, rent received for agricultural land also a few examples of agricultural income.
However, this type of agricultural income – in scenarios where it is the only source of income – is not taxable in India. We offer expert services in planning, building, and benefitting from income in this category.

Tax on Other Incomes
We offer services in the areas of savings bank interest, family pension and so on that comprises other sources of income.

Income Tax Filings
We provide Income Tax filing services to Individuals, HUF, Firms, Corporates, etc. Our filing services enables Individuals to ensure that compliance of law is carried out to perfection and hence avoiding any hardships in the future.

GST

A new, multistage tax structure introduced by Government of India, Goods and Services Tax (GST) is an umbrella tax which replaces a slew of indirect taxes such as central excise duty, duties of excise, additional duties of excise, additional duties of customs, special additional duty of customs, CESS, state vat, central sales tax, etc. GST, however is still in an evolution stage and presents a lot of new challenges to businesses from time to time. In order to ensure that your business can overcome these challenges and ensure that day to day transactions are carried out in a complaint yet profitable manner we provide a slew of services:

  1. GST Advisory Services – We ensure that your business is equipped to cope up with the requirements of the ever-evolving GST law and thus avoid any losses which may occur later due to non-compliance related issues.
  2. GST Compliance Services – We at Penny worth help your business with the umpteen GST compliances on a routine business thus reducing the time spent on such non- core activities and ensuring accurate filing of returns which reduces litigations in the long run.
  3. GST Representation Services – In case of receipt of any notices from authorities we provide appropriate replies and also represent your case against authorities at various forums such as Tax Officers, Appeallate Commissioner, Tribunal, etc
