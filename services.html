Audit and Advisory<

Audit assists in the establishment of ‘best practices’ which in turn leads to sound internal controls within the company. Audit also aids in enhancing the efficiency in operations which leads to performance improvement and profit of the company. Audit helps businesses in multiple ways. Notable among those are mentioned below.

  • Adds value in solving the issues faced by businesses by providing insight on the effectiveness of governance, risk management, and internal control processes.
  • Post-audit recommendations help in fortifying controls, enabling efficient bookkeeping and building proficient accounting practices.
  • Depicts a clear picture of the prevailing financial situation of the company and provides valuable insights on future scenario which can prepare companies to handle upcoming conditions in a much better way.
  • Helps in gaining investor confidence as audit report is a valuable tool for financial institutions and investment houses to estimate the valuation of a business firm.

At Penny Worth Consultants, our range of audit advisory services includes:
Internal audits: Internal audit services empower companies in safeguarding of assets and all key matters involving the interest of the company, and its stakeholders. In addition, our internal audit services are designed to add value and improve operational efficiency of businesses. We help in bringing in a systematic approach to evaluating the efficacy of existing risk management and governance processes.
Operational audit: Our operational audit services involve systematic evaluation of the overall functioning of an organization, including all internal processes and procedures. We help our client companies achieve optimal utilization of the available resources to maximize profits. The services are customized to the needs of the business of clients and help checking the efficiency of operational structures.
Operational audit services at Penny Worth Consultants focuses on the streamlining and optimizing business resources, key processes, procedures, and system, to improve productivity, efficiency and effectiveness of the operation.
